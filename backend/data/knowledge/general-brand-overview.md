## BMW brand overview (demo)

BMW is commonly positioned as a premium brand that blends everyday usability with a sporty driving character. In practice, buyers compare BMW models against other premium marques when choosing between comfort-oriented setups and more driver-focused trims.

This demo knowledge file is intentionally generic. It exists to give the retrieval system "global" context that is not tied to a single vehicle slug.

When reading BMW marketing language, treat claims as orientation, not guarantees. Real-world experience depends on road conditions, tyres, maintenance history, and the exact options fitted to a car.
