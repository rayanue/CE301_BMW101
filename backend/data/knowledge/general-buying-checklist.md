## Used BMW buying checklist (demo)

When evaluating a used BMW, buyers typically look for consistent service history, evidence of timely fluid changes, and whether common wear items (brakes, tyres, suspension bushings) were addressed.

A short test drive can reveal alignment issues, vibration under braking, and abnormal drivetrain noises. Cold-start behaviour and warning lights should be noted, even if they appear intermittent.

This checklist is educational only. It does not replace a professional inspection or manufacturer guidance for a specific vehicle.
