## Insurance and running costs (high level, demo)

Insurance premiums depend on driver profile, postcode, annual mileage, security, and the vehicle group. Premium-brand vehicles can be more expensive to insure because repair costs and parts pricing are often higher.

Fuel or energy costs depend on driving patterns. Short urban cycles tend to reduce real-world efficiency for any powertrain, while steady motorway cruising often improves it.

Use these notes as a framework for questions, not as quotes or guarantees.
