## BMW naming basics (demo)

BMW model names often combine a series number with body style cues and powertrain badges. Higher series numbers often (but not always) correlate with larger vehicles and higher price positioning.

Badges like "i", "d", and "e" are commonly used shorthand in BMW communities. In modern lineups, "e" may indicate electrified variants depending on the generation and market.

For dissertation demos, treat naming as a navigation aid, not a complete specification of equipment. Always cross-check equipment lists for the exact vehicle you are considering.
