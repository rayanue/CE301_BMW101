## Warranty and servicing concepts (demo)

Many buyers separate "scheduled servicing" from "repairs". Servicing is the maintenance plan intended to keep systems within expected operating parameters. Repairs address failures or wear outside normal expectations.

Dealer servicing and independent specialist servicing each have trade-offs: dealers may have factory tooling and update pathways, while independents may offer lower labour rates.
