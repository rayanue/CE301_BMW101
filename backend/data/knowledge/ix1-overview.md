## BMW iX1 (demo overview)

The iX1 is often discussed as a compact SUV-style BMW with a battery-electric powertrain in many markets. For this dissertation demo, treat all numbers as illustrative placeholders rather than official manufacturer claims.

### What buyers typically like

Buyers who want a higher seating position sometimes choose compact SUVs because entry/exit can feel easier than a low saloon. Electric powertrains can feel responsive in urban driving because torque delivery can be immediate at typical city speeds.

Packaging matters: rear seat comfort, boot shape, and cabin storage can matter more day-to-day than headline performance figures.

### Practical considerations (demo)

Charging behaviour depends on home charging access, workplace charging, and public network availability. If you cannot charge at home, an EV may still work, but it requires more planning.

Tyres and wheels affect range and road noise. Larger wheels can look sporty but may reduce efficiency and increase replacement costs.

### Questions to ask a seller (demo)
