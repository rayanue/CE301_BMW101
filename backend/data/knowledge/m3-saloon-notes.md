## BMW M3 (m3): high-performance saloon / touring lineage

Historically, **M3** denotes the **M division's four-door (and sometimes touring) performance** variant of the 3 Series architecture - **engineered for track-capable dynamics** with saloon practicality. Demo slug: **(m3)**.

### Saloon advantages vs coupe

Four doors improve **rear passenger access** and **professional "daily driver" usability** while retaining M-tuned suspension, steering, and braking philosophy (market and generation dependent).

### Running costs framing

Expect **premium fuel**, **performance tyres**, and **more frequent consumables** than a 320i-class car. Long motorway stints are comfortable; short urban cycles may show weaker real-world mpg.

### Buyer questions (coursework)

- Intended split of **commute vs spirited driving**
- **Wheel and tyre package** (winter wheel sets are a common UK discussion)
- **Warranty and approved-used** programmes when buying pre-owned
