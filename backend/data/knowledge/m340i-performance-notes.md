## BMW M340i (demo): performance-oriented saloon notes

The M340i is often used as an example of a "high-performance daily driver" within a saloon body style. For coursework, treat it as a case study in trade-offs: stronger acceleration potential vs higher running costs.

### Tyres and consumables (demo)

Performance trims may ship with wider, grippier tyres that wear faster and cost more. Brake pads may also be sized for higher thermal loads, which can influence replacement pricing.

### Comfort vs focus (conceptual)

Sport suspension packages can improve handling feel but may transmit more road texture into the cabin. Buyers sensitive to ride comfort should validate on roads they use weekly.

### Academic angle

You can map "performance_score" in your recommender to qualitative claims, then discuss limitations: subjective comfort cannot be fully captured by a 1-5 scalar.
