## BMW M4 (m4): M4 coupe and convertible family

**M4** usually maps to the **two-door (and sometimes convertible)** high-performance counterpart to M3 saloon engineering - same broad "M division" philosophy with **coupe packaging**. Canonical id for this repo: **(m4)**.

### Packaging vs M3

Rear-seat and boot access patterns resemble other **two-door performance cars**: style and chassis focus often beat ease of rear entry. Compare explicitly to **m3** in buyer conversations.

### Tyres, alignment, and wear

Staggered wheel setups and aggressive camber specs (generation-dependent) can affect **tyre inner-edge wear**. Alignment checks matter after pothole strikes.

### Use-case segmentation

- **Primary driver, occasional rear passengers:** workable if expectations are set.
- **Primary family car with frequent child loading:** often a weaker fit than hatch/SUV/saloon M3.
