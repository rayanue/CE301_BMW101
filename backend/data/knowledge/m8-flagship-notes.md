## BMW M8 (m8): flagship M positioning

**M8** represents BMW M's **top-tier performance GT** positioning - often **coupe, convertible, or four-door Gran Coupe** variants depending on generation and market. This demo uses a single slug **(m8)** for all M8-tagged narrative.

### Grand touring vs pure track tool

Expect emphasis on **high-speed stability**, **refinement**, and **long-distance comfort** compared with smaller M cars, while still carrying **very strong performance credentials**. Mass and width change the driving character versus M2/M4.

### Seating and usability

Many M8 bodies are **2+2 or prioritise front occupants**; treat **family_score** cautiously in a recommender unless the buyer explicitly wants a flagship weekend car.

### Running costs (high level)

Flagship tyres, brakes, and insurance assumptions typically sit **above** M3/M4 tiers; service parts pricing often follows vehicle segment as well as badge.
