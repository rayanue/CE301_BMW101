## BMW X7 (demo): seven-seat SUV considerations

Seven-seat vehicles are useful when you occasionally need extra seats, but buyers should validate whether the third row suits the people you carry (legroom, headroom, and ease of access).

### Practical trade-offs (demo)

Larger vehicles can be harder to park and may feel wide on narrow streets. On the other hand, they can offer strong stability on motorways and a commanding view of the road.

### Trip planning (conceptual)

If you regularly carry six or seven passengers, also consider luggage volume with all seats upright. Some configurations require folding seats to carry bulky luggage.
